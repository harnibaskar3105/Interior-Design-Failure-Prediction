Project Overview

This system evaluates interior design parameters to identify potential design failures in smart homes. It analyzes factors such as room type, material selection, lighting conditions, and space configuration to detect issues like improper layout planning, poor illumination, and reduced energy efficiency.

Key Features

  • Design failure prediction based on predefined rules and logic.
  • Analysis of lighting conditions and space utilization.
  • Evaluation of material selection and functional efficiency.
  • Simple and user-friendly interface for easy interaction.

Get Started

Begin your interior design evaluation by entering key parameters such as room type, material selection, lighting conditions, and spatial configuration.
